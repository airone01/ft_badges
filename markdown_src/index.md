**ft_badges** is a dynamic, fully customizable SVG badge generator designed
specifically for 42 School projects.

Whether you want to show off your perfect 125% score or just add some visual
flair to your GitHub profile, ft_badges generates clean, responsive, and
beautifully themed SVGs that you can drop directly into your `README.md`.

## Features

- **Multiple Themes:** Choose between `dark` and `light` modes to match your
  profile's aesthetic.
- **Styling Variants:** Opt for the clean `classic` look or the textured `noisy`
  variant for a more retro feel.
- **Dynamic Colors:** Badge accent colors automatically shift based on the
  project and pass/fail thresholds.
- **Custom Layouts:** Pick between a sleek 42 logo or a bracketed text style
  `[ 42 ]`.

## Getting Started

Ready to upgrade your README?

1. Check out the **[Usage Guide](usage.md)** to learn how to construct your
   badge URLs.
2. Browse the **Projects** in the sidebar to copy pre-generated Markdown
   snippets for specific assignments like `libft` or `minishell`.
